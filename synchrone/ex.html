FAQ

What is Elevar?

Elevar is a national-level techfest, conducted by the members of Lendi Institute of Engineering and Technology.

When and where is Elevar being held?

Elevar 2K18 is a 2 day event and will be conducted on 9th January and 10th January, 2018 at Lendi Institute of Engineering and Technology in Jonnada, Vizianagaram.

Who can participate?

The participants must be pursuing their undergraduate degree in a recognized institution. There is no restriction on the stream of study of the participants.

What kind of events will the participants be involved in during the fest?

This fest is a set of presentations, competitions and hackathon.

What is the registration process?

Those who are interested to participate in the events of the fest need to provide the necessary details in the registration portal of this website.

Is there a fee for participation in the fest?

Elevar is an exclusive event, and all selected participants will be required to pay Rs. 500.

How will I know of my selection?

All selected participants will be sent emails regarding selection. The applicants are requested to stay connected through email for all communication during the registration process.

What does this fest offer its participants?

This fest provides a chance for conscientious individuals to interact with like minded people and professionals in the field. Moreover, all participants will be awarded a certificate for attending the fest. Also, there are cash prizes for every winner and runners of each event.

Would accommodation and food be provided to the participants during the event?

Participants will be provided with food and accommodation at a nominal price. This is over and above the registration fee after selection, and has to be paid before hand.

Will it be required for participants to come to Lendi any time before the fest?

Participants are not required to come to the college before the fest. But, they need to register online and pay the fee prior to the deadline of registration.
